Dataset Information

This dashboard provides a comprehensive exploration of UFC fight data from 1994 to 2025. It brings together key statistics, performance metrics, and historical insights to help identify patterns across fighters, events, and outcomes. By analyzing trends such as fight frequency, methods of victory, and head-to-head rivalries, the goal is to create a clearer picture of how the sport has evolved over time and highlight factors that influence success inside the octagon.
